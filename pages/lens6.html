Half-Life was full of crazy and huge enemies but for an important moment of thinking nothing better comes to mind then the tentacle monster found in the test launch chamber. After the player attempts to go to the surface for help, he encounters soldiers who instead of helping are killing everything in their way, the player heads back into the black mesa facility and soon encounters a massive creature that uses hearing to attack.

The player is giving quite a few grenades after a soldier from black mesa tells the player the monster is using sounds so be quiet, my first thoughts were just to be quiet and sneak through but many of the doorways of ladder have objects in the way, using the crowbar would be too loud and take too long. Players quickly realize they could use the grenade loud explosion to break the blockage and distract the monster, this plan works and the player makes it pass the monster.
