One important moment was soon after the player kills the large tentacle monster they are making their way to and old passageway and inside of this huge room there is a monster probably 20ft tall and shooting lasers or something from its arms during this moment the player has no chance of shooting it and killing it as it rips through your health at an extremely fast pace. However, the player must get past it to continue the game so running across the large room into a small one the monster can't fit through.

During this whole encounter the player is at first surprised, this is the second large monster in their way but way more deadly than when they don’t see any choice but to fight it the player is shocked by how much damage the monster does, the player decides to just blitz it but is scared the plan is dumb and will fail. All these different emotions raise the tension in a game although it could seem like unwanted stress this is action-adventure game so if fighting the monster didn’t work the play has to hope there is some puzzle to beat the monster so he can press on, this whole unexpected fight with something too strong can be very exciting for the player and make them understand the power dynamic the game is building.
