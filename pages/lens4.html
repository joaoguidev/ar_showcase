Lens of Sensation

Back

Well, the first Half-life is full of neat little traps placed all around you that you have to navigate around using your many senses to notice in the first place, although mostly just sight to be honest. For example, quite early on in the game you come across a corridor that bends to the right which has an alarm laser on the floor right before the corridor turns, water on the floor, and an open elevator shaft. By analyzing the terrain you see that by jumping over the laser alarm normally you will slip on the water and fall down the shaft. So, because you used your amazing sense of sight, you are able to plan ahead, shift a bit to the right and then when you jump and then slip you slam into the wall next to the elevator door rather than falling through it. Hurrah for eyes!
