One encounter that stands out in the original Half-life is the first time you come across a member of the military. The game spends a great while setting up the anxiety of the Black Mesa staff about whether theyâ€™ll be able to escape the facility and the hope they have that the military will come in and save them. That makes the moment when you first meet a soldier as they are in the process of killing a soldier that shows you their hope was misplaced. You now have seconds to react as what you thought would be a bastion of hope becomes an enemy that you must now kill in order to survive. This encounter ends the first act of the Half-life, introduces a new enemy type, and establishes a theme of justified mistrust towards authority that will carry over to the sequel.
